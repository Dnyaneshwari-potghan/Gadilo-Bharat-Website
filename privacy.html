GARDILO - Privacy Policy
At GADILO Bharat, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, which enables multiple dealers to showcase their products (Cars/Bikes/Accessories) to customers online. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.
We collect your following during registration
- To contact you regarding your account, listings, or changes to our policies
- To validate your identity and confirm your eligibility as a dealer on GADILO Bharat.
Note- We do not sell or share your personal or business information with third parties.
As a dealer, you have the rights:
- To Review your personal or business information through your account profile.
- To update your contact number through Adhar verification ONLY.
- To delete or update your product listings as per your choice.
- To delete/deactivate your account at any time (Refund Policy)
What information we share with our users (Public):
- Product details you upload (e.g., car, bike, spare part, or accessory listings) are visible to all platform users(public). This includes product images, specifications, prices, and other relevant information.
- Your business name and your profile picture.
- Trusted third-party providers (our service providers) may access your information to support our platform operations, such as payment processing or identity verification.
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Dealers will be notified of significant updates via email or platform notifications.
We implement robust measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes:
- Secure storage and encryption of sensitive data.
- Restricted access to authorized personnel only.
- Regular security audits of our systems.
However, no system is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
